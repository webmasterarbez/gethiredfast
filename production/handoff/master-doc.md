## Production Requirements

### Presenter

**Cat Breet**
- Role: Course presenter and subject matter expert
- Background: 22 years recruiting and career coaching
- Tone: Warm, direct, encouraging, no-nonsense
- Energy: Conversational, not academic

### Video Production

**Format**
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Aspect ratio: 16:9

**Shot Types**
- Talking head (80%): Medium shot, eye to camera
- Screencast (15%): 1920x1080 screen capture
- Graphics/B-roll (5%): Full-screen overlays

**Equipment**
- Camera: DSLR/mirrorless with clean HDMI out
- Audio: Lavalier microphone
- Lighting: 3-point setup
- Teleprompter: Scripts provided

### Audio Production

**Voiceover**
- Format: WAV 48kHz/24-bit
- Noise floor: -60dB or lower
- Peak level: -6dB to -3dB

**Music**
- Royalty-free or licensed
- Intro/outro: 5-8 seconds
- Background: -20dB to -25dB under voice

## Assessment Strategy

### Philosophy
Portfolio-based assessment — every deliverable is immediately usable in the student's real job search.

### Portfolio Components

| Deliverable | Module | Rubric |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| Tailored Resume | M2 | rubric-resume.yaml |
| LinkedIn Profile | M2 | rubric-linkedin.yaml |
| STAR Story Bank (5+) | M4 | rubric-star-stories.yaml |
| Network Map (50+) | M3 | rubric-network-map.yaml |
| 90-Day Action Plan | M7 | rubric-action-plan.yaml |
| Daily Action Tracker | M0 | checklist only |

### Knowledge Checks
- Quiz 0: Course Orientation (5 questions)
- Quiz 1: Career Direction (8 questions)
- Quiz 2: Personal Brand (10 questions)
- Quiz 3: Networking (8 questions)
- Quiz 4: Interviews (10 questions)
- Quiz 5: Negotiation (8 questions)

### Self-Assessments
Confidence ratings (1-5 scale) after each module with reflection prompts.

### Feedback Collection
- Module completion surveys (after each module)
- Course completion survey with NPS
- 30/60/90-day outcome surveys

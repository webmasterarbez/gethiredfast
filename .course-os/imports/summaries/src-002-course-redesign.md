# Source Summary: Career Success Course Redesign 2025-2026

**Source ID:** src-002
**Type:** Planning Document
**Priority:** Critical

## Overview

Comprehensive redesign specification for modernizing the career course for the AI-era job market (2025-2026). Proposes three audience-specific tracks while preserving proven foundational content.

## Three Audience Tracks

### Track A: Career Launchpad (Community College Students)
**THIS IS OUR FOCUS**

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Demographics | Ages 18-30, completing associate degrees/certificates |
| Prior Knowledge | Limited professional experience, some part-time work |
| Technical Proficiency | High social media fluency, moderate professional tools |
| Learning Context | Mobile-first, time-constrained, working while studying |
| Motivations | First "real" job, financial independence, career direction |
| Barriers | Imposter syndrome, thin resume, limited network |

**Duration:** 5 Weeks (45-60 minutes/week)

**Modules:**
1. Career GPS - Find Your Direction
2. Build Your Brand - Tell Your Story
3. Find Opportunities - Work Your Network
4. Interview Like a Pro - Land the Offer
5. Launch Your Career - Negotiate & Start Strong

### Track B: Career Catalyst (Mid-Career)
- Ages 30-55, 5-20+ years experience
- Focus on AI adaptation, ageism, network reactivation
- Duration: 6 weeks

### Track C: Tech Career Navigator
- Technology professionals
- Focus on coding interviews, system design, equity negotiation
- Duration: 5 weeks

## Learning Outcomes Framework

Uses Bloom's Taxonomy across all levels:

**Remember/Understand:**
- Identify 5 components of AI-optimized job search
- Describe how ATS and AI screening evaluate candidates
- List 8 human skills AI cannot replicate

**Apply:**
- Use ChatGPT/Claude to tailor resumes
- Apply STAR storytelling to behavioral interviews
- Execute 30-day networking campaign

**Analyze:**
- Compare job offers using total compensation framework
- Evaluate personal AI exposure risk
- Assess networking strategies by conversion rate

**Evaluate:**
- Critique resume drafts for AI/human readability
- Determine appropriate salary negotiation range
- Judge cultural fit indicators

**Create:**
- Design personal brand positioning
- Develop AI-enhanced job search workflow
- Build 90-day career resilience plan

## Assessment Strategy

### Kirkpatrick Levels
- **Level 1 (Reaction):** Post-module surveys, NPS
- **Level 2 (Learning):** Pre/post assessments, deliverables
- **Level 3 (Behavior):** Job search activities, profile updates
- **Level 4 (Results):** Job offer rate, time to employment, salary outcomes

### Target Metrics
- Course completion: 75%+
- Satisfaction (NPS): 50+
- Job offer within 90 days: 60%+
- Salary at or above target: 70%+

## Key Deliverables for Students

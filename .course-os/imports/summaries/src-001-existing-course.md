**Source ID:** src-001

## Overview

A comprehensive 6-week, 145-video career curriculum created by career coach Cat Breet, who brings 22+ years of recruiting experience. The course promises to help job seekers find employment 55% faster than average (10 weeks vs 21.6 weeks national average).

## Course Structure

### Week 0: Welcome (5 videos)
- Course introduction and orientation
- Success stories and testimonials
- "Zebra Joe" metaphor for standing out

### Week 1: Isolate Your Target (22 videos)
- Self-assessment and skills inventory
- Mindset and gratitude exercises
- Finding champions and support systems
- Career exploration exercises
- Target job identification
- Overcoming barriers and fears

### Week 2: Stand Out & Tell Your Story (27 videos)
- Resume development (7+ videos)
- Cover letter writing
- LinkedIn optimization (10 videos)
- Personal branding
- STAR storytelling method
- Transferable skills identification

### Week 3: Find Jobs & Get Past Gatekeepers (20 videos)
- Hidden job market (80% unpublished)
- Networking strategies and scripts
- Referral strategies
- Online application optimization
- ATS (Applicant Tracking System) navigation
- Social media job search

### Week 4: Interview Success (32 videos)
- Interview preparation framework
- Company and people research
- Common and "scary" questions
- Video interview mastery
- Recruiter vs. hiring manager strategies
- Behavioral interview techniques (STAR)
- Follow-up and reference management

### Week 5: Money Talk (13 videos)
- Salary research methodology
- Understanding total compensation
- W2 vs 1099 considerations
- Negotiation preparation
- Scripts for asking and countering
- Offer comparison framework

### Week 6: Break Through Barriers (16 videos)
- Discrimination awareness and strategies
- Ageism (too young and too old)
- Inner critic management
- Limiting beliefs identification
- Courage and confidence building

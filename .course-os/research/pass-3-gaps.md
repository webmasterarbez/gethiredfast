## Critical Gap 2: AI Video Interview Preparation

### Research Status: ✅ COMPLETE

### Key Findings

**70% of companies plan to use AI for hiring by 2025**, with HireVue leading.

### What AI Interviews Measure

| Factor | What's Analyzed |
|--------|-----------------|
| Communication | Clarity, conciseness, language quality |
| Non-verbal | Facial expressions, eye contact, gestures |
| Voice | Pace, intonation, modulation |
| Body language | Posture, hand movements |
| Content | Job-relevant skills, experience alignment |

### Format (Typical)
- 3-5 pre-recorded questions
- 30 seconds to prepare
- Up to 3 minutes to answer (aim for 90 seconds)
- Some allow retakes, many do not

### Preparation Tips

**Technical:**
- Neutral background
- Camera at eye level
- Key light at 45°, above eye level
- Hardwired internet if possible
- Test webcam/mic beforehand

**Answer Structure:**
- Use STAR/SOAR method
- Keep under 90 seconds
- Practice with platform's practice questions

**Presentation:**
- Dress as for in-person interview
- Maintain eye contact with CAMERA (not screen)
- Positive energy and expression

### Key Warning
> "There's no course that will help you game an AI assessment. Focus on genuine preparation."

**Recommended Videos:**
- "AI Interviews Explained: What's Really Being Measured"
- "HireVue and One-Way Interviews: How to Succeed"
- "Technical Setup for Video Interviews"

## Critical Gap 3: AI Impact on Career Choices

### Research Status: ✅ COMPLETE

### Key Findings

**Entry-level is being hit hard:**
- Hiring down 16% vs 2024, 44% below 2022
- Underemployment at 41.8% (highest since 2020)
- College degree advantage is narrowing

**AI is changing the landscape:**
- Many companies assessing AI integration
- Some entry-level roles being automated
- AI skills now command 28% salary premium

### Growing Fields (Despite AI)
- Healthcare (aging population)
- Engineering
- Technology (especially AI-adjacent roles)
- Skilled trades

### Skills AI Can't Replace

| Skill | Why It Matters |
|-------|----------------|
| Creative thinking | Novel solutions, innovation |
| Resilience & flexibility | Adapting to change |
| Curiosity & learning | Continuous growth |
| Emotional intelligence | Relationships, collaboration |
| Critical judgment | Evaluating AI output |
| Ethical reasoning | Value-based decisions |

### AI-Proof Career Strategy
1. Build AI literacy (know how to use AI tools)
2. Develop human skills AI lacks
3. Focus on roles requiring judgment, creativity, relationships
4. Stay adaptable and continue learning

## Critical Gap 4: Modern ATS/AI Screening

### Research Status: ✅ COMPLETE

### Key Findings

- **97.8%** of Fortune 500 use ATS
- **75%+** of resumes rejected before human review
- Modern ATS uses NLP and AI for context understanding

### What's Changed in 2025
- AI can understand context, not just keywords
- Keyword stuffing is now detected and penalized
- Natural writing matters more than ever
- Need both human AND machine optimization

### Optimization Strategy

**Formatting:**
- Simple, single-column layout
- No tables, graphics, text boxes
- Standard fonts
- .docx format preferred
- Contact info in body (not header/footer)

**Keywords:**
- Mirror exact job posting language
- Place in Skills section + experience bullets
- Use naturally, not stuffed
- Target 75%+ Jobscan match rate

**Tools:**
- Jobscan (free scans available)
- Hireflow (free)
- Resume Worded (freemium)

### Gap Resolution
**Recommended Content:**
- Update existing ATS videos with 2025 strategies
- Add "Using Jobscan to Test Your Resume"
- Cover AI-powered screening vs traditional ATS

## High Gap 6: First-Generation Student Support

### Research Status: ✅ COMPLETE

### Key Findings

- **35%** of first-gen students never use career services
- **70%** of jobs come through networking (first-gen lack networks)
- **80%** of jobs are never advertised (hidden job market)

### Specific Challenges

| Challenge | Impact |
|-----------|--------|
| No professional network | Miss 70%+ of opportunities |
| Lack of mentors | No guidance on professional norms |
| Imposter syndrome | Undermines confidence |
| Limited career awareness | Only know traditional paths |
| Financial pressure | Stress from debt |

### Successful Programs
- CSU Fullerton "I Am First" — mentorship + microinternships
- Grinnell College — advisor assigned to all first-years
- Hamilton "First Year Forward" — year-long bridge program

### Strategies for First-Gen Students
1. **Use career services early** — they exist to help you
2. **Build network from scratch** — professors, classmates, online
3. **Learn professional norms** — dress, communication, etiquette
4. **Fight imposter syndrome** — you belong, you earned it
5. **Seek mentorship** — formal programs or informal connections

### Gap Resolution
**Recommended Videos:**
- "First-Gen Student? This Is For You"
- "Building a Network From Zero"
- "Professional Norms Nobody Told You"
